How To Use Qure’s Micro-Infusion System
In 5 Simple Steps.

Qure’s Micro-Infusion System is simple and intuitive to use.

STEP 1:

Twist open the chamber of the device and
fill with one ampule of serum. Twist on
needle head with lid to close it tight.

STEP 2:

Leave the device upside down for 1-2
minutes. During this time, wash your face
and make sure your hands are clean.

STEP 3:

Once the serum has had enough time to
make its way into the needles, you can
take off the lid and start micro-infusing!

STEP 4:

Start at the centre of the face and work
your way outward to cover the full face.
Take any leftover serum from the device
and pat it onto your face and neck to leave
on overnight.

STEP 5:

Once treatment is completed, place lid
back on the needle head. Twist the lid off
and dispose safely. Open a new sterile
needle head and put it on the device ready
for your next treatment.
